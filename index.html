About the Foundation

We are The Weintraub, a non-profit organization established in 2024 with a mission to make a meaningful impact on mental health.

Our core belief is that by fostering awareness, understanding, and unity, we can create a supportive environment to conquer the challenges associated with mental health. Through collaborative efforts, we strive to bring people and communities together, offering aid, resources, and a platform for open dialogue. Join us in our journey to make a positive difference in the lives affected by mental health issues and contribute to building a world where mental well-being is prioritized and embraced. Together, we can make strides towards a healthier and more compassionate future.

Aid Targets

The Weintraub Foundation is still in it's infancy but is expanding at a rapid pace and consisting of a few options for those in need of our services. Below, you will find a few of our offerings with more to come in the future!

Anxiety

Excessive worry, fear, or nervousness, frequently accompanied by physical symptoms such as restlessness, rapid heart rate, excessive sweating, and muscle tension.

Depression

Persistent feelings of sadness, hopelessness, and a lack of interest or pleasure in daily activities, potentially leading to physical symptoms like changes in sleep and appetite.

Schizophrenia

A severe mental disorder involving distorted thinking, hallucinations, and impaired social functioning, often accompanied by disorganized speech and behavior.

Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder

A mental health condition triggered by experiencing or witnessing a traumatic event, leading to persistent distressing memories, flashbacks, and emotional numbness.

Eating Disorder

A range of mental health conditions characterized by unhealthy eating habits and preoccupations with body weight and shape, such as anorexia nervosa, bulimia nervosa, and binge-eating disorder.

Suicide Prevention

The collective efforts and strategies aimed at reducing the risk of suicide, including but not limited to awareness, intervention, and support to individuals experiencing thoughts of self-harm.

About The Founder

"Jamie's wise quote goes here"

Jamie Weintraub

is a distinguished country musician and former runner-up on the reality TV show "The Bachelor." Beyond her entertainment career, Jamie serves as an analyst at Cerberus, a world-leading private equity firm. Despite her professional success, Jamie’s journey took a profound turn when she grappled with her own mental health challenges. Fueled by her own experiences, Jamie founded The Weintraub in 2024, driven by the belief that understanding and compassion are crucial in addressing mental health concerns. Her unique background as both a talented artist and a professional in the financial sector reflects the diverse perspectives she brings to our mission. Through The Weintraub, Jamie aims to leverage her influence to raise awareness, eliminate stigma, and provide vital support to those navigating the complexities of mental health.
